Vinegar Rice Recipe

    Ingredients
  • Rinse 3 cups of rice
  • Add 3 cups of water
  • 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  • 3 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 salt
    Instructions
  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Rinse the 3 cups of rice
  3. Put rinsed rice and water to cook with rice cooker
  4. After making steamed rice, transfer the rice into the bowl
  5. While put in the sushi vinegar ( mixed with vinegar, sugar, and salt)
  6. Cooling down the sushi rice gently flip the rice in between slices repeatedly until it is cooled
  7. Keep the rice covered with a damp towel for without dry

Sushi Roll Recipe

    Ingredients
  • Some Sushi rice
  • A main ingredients Tuna, Cucumber, Avocado, Japanese pickles.
  • Some Nori (sea weed)
  • Bamboo sushi rolling mat
  • Soy sause
    Instructions
    Tuna, Cucumber, and Avocado :
  1. Prepare Sushi Rice
  2. Cut both ends of the cucumber in 1/4 lengthwise to have 4 strips.
  3. Cut the tuna into 1/4-1/2” slices and cut into 1/4-1/2” hick long strips.
  4. Cut half avocado and take out the seed.
  5. 1/4 slice the avocado
  6. Make vinegared hand-dipping water by combining 1/4 cup water and 1 Tbsp rice vinegar in a small bowl for using to prevents rice form sticking to your hands.
  7. Cut nori in half
  8. Place the sushi mat on a working surface and put the nori half sheet on the mat. The shiny side of nori should face down.
  9. Moisten your hands before you touch sushi rice
  10. Scoop the amount of sushi rice on the bamboo mat flat to spread evelny.
  11. place the filling tuna, cucumber or avocado at he middle of rice.
  12. Roll the mat over the sushi rice gently shape and tighten.
  13. Cut a sushi roll with wetting your knife.

Fish Grill Recipe

    Ingredients
  • Any kinds of fish
  • Salt
Instructions
  • Take off the scales of the fish skin with knife
  • Cut fish stomach for taking out the internal organs with rinsing water
  • Sprinkle some salt on the fish
  • Grill the fish until done
